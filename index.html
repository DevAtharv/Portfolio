About Me

I’m a passionate educator specializing in Commerce, with a strong academic foundation that includes a Ph.D. in Commerce, M.Com, and B.Com. My teaching philosophy is centered on clarity, discipline, and helping students understand concepts in a way that feels practical and empowering.

Over the years, I have focused on making Business Studies and Economics approachable for learners by connecting theory with real classroom application. My work has involved not only regular teaching but also mentoring, lesson design, and academic guidance for students preparing for key school milestones.

Beyond academics, my Kathak training has shaped the rhythm, patience, and expression I bring into my teaching practice. I value learning environments that are structured, encouraging, and deeply human.
